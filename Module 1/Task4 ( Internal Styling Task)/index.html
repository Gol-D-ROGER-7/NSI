About NexusTech

Established in 1958, the second of its kind, NexusTech was the first to be set up with foreign assistance. The funds from UNESCO came as Roubles from the then Soviet Union. In 1961 Parliament decreed the IITs as ‘Institutes of National Importance'. Since then, NexusTech has grown from strength to strength to emerge as one of the top technical universities in the world.
